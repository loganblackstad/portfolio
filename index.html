Featured Project: DigitalTasks

DigitalTasks is a task management chrome extension that aggregates both personal and business tasks (via our Salesforce integration). The app allows users to create, update, and delete tasks and contacts from almost any website. Using the app's filter feature, users can quickly view overdue, completed, and upcoming tasks sorted by date. Additionally, DigitalTasks directly integrates with Salesforce, allowing users to easily view, update, and delete tasks without logging into their Salesforce instance.
